The 21st edition of the Klangtherapie Festival is fast approaching!

We are looking forward to four days of TECHNO - LOVE - UTOPIA with you.

“The freedom to be who we are”

For 22 years now, our field has been transformed into a playground of endless possibilities on the first weekend in August in Franconian Switzerland. For four days, all facets of electronic music, concerts, workshops, lectures, performances, wellness and much more are presented non-stop on five individually designed stages.

Away from commerce, miles away from the mainstream, but very close to our utopia. Let yourself be catapulted out of everyday life on our playground and arrive where you have always wanted to be: IN THE FREEDOM TO BE WHO YOU ARE!

Get your ticket now!

Our festival should be accessible to everyone. That's why there are many ways in which you can become part of our utopia. Our Early Bird or standard ticket is the admission ticket for normal earners. By purchasing a solidarity ticket, you subsidize the minimum ticket for low-income earners. If there are five of you, you can save €75 with our 5-person group ticket.

Would you like to support us as a volunteer? Then give us your power and we'll give you a ticket. You can find information about helping here. With the Kulturpass we offer tickets for young adults. Children's tickets are valid from 3-15 years. Children from 0-2 years are free. From 16 years full price.
Day tickets will be available in the store from April 1st.
